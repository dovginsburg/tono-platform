features

four ways tono has your back

tap the coach button, pick a tone, and tono rewrites your message on the spot.

warmer

soften sharp edges. add warmth without losing your point — perfect for saying no gracefully.

clearer

cut the fluff. make your point land in one read — no more back-and-forth clarification.

funnier

land a laugh without trying too hard. light wit, dry humor, or playful energy — your call.

safer

catch the message you'd regret before it ships. tone-safety check before every send.

how it works

three taps. that's it.

no new app to open. tono lives in your keyboard, right where you already type.

1

type

write the message the way it first came out — the raw, unfiltered version.

2

tap coach

hit the tono button. pick a tone — warmer, clearer, funnier, or safer.

3

pick your rewrite

tono suggests a few options. pick the one that sounds like you. send.
