Tono is an AI-powered keyboard coach that helps you say what you mean and land how you intend.

Before you hit send, Tono analyzes your message and offers four rewrites:
• Warmer – soften your tone without losing your point
• Clearer – cut confusion and say it plainly  
• Funnier – add levity when the moment calls for it
• Safer – avoid unintended offense

HOW IT WORKS
1. Enable the Tono keyboard in Settings
2. Type your message in any app
3. Tap "Coach" to get instant tone analysis
4. Choose a rewrite or keep your original

WHO IT'S FOR
Tono is designed for anyone who's ever sent a message and thought "that came out wrong." Whether you're navigating workplace communication, managing relationships, or just want to be more intentional with your words – Tono helps you pause, reflect, and choose better.

PRIVACY
Your messages are analyzed in real-time and never stored on our servers. Tono processes text through secure AI services and discards it immediately after generating suggestions.
