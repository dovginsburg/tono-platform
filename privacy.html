Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Tono ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Tono mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our Service.

Information We Collect

Text Analysis

When you use the Tono keyboard, your drafted text is sent to our servers for tone analysis. We do not store your messages after analysis. Text is processed in real-time and immediately discarded.

Account Information

If you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address (for authentication)
  • Device identifier (for rate limiting)

Usage Data

We collect anonymous usage statistics:

  • Number of rewrites used per day
  • Feature usage patterns (which axes you select)
  • Crash reports (no personal content)

Payment Information

Subscription payments are handled by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We do not have access to your credit card information.

How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain our Service
  • To analyze text tone and provide rewrites (real-time only, not stored)
  • To track usage for free tier limits
  • To improve the app through anonymous analytics

Data Storage

  • Text sent for analysis is processed in real-time and not persisted
  • Account data is stored securely on our servers
  • Usage statistics are anonymized and aggregated

Third-Party Services

  • Anthropic — Used for AI-powered tone analysis. Their privacy policy applies to data sent during analysis.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store — Handle payments and subscription management

Data Retention

  • Analysis text: Discarded immediately after processing
  • Account data: Retained while account is active
  • Usage data: Retained for 12 months, then deleted

Your Rights

  • Delete your account: Contact us at privacy@tonoit.com
  • Export your data: Contact us at privacy@tonoit.com
  • Opt out of analytics: Disable in Settings → Privacy

Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@tonoit.com
  • Website: tonoit.com
