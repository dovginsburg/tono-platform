PRODUCTION NOTES FOR ALL FIVE:
  - Realistic-looking chat content (use plausible names, plausible situations) — never "Lorem ipsum"
  - The Tono Coach card UI must match the actual built UI exactly — no aspirational mock-ups that don't match what's shipping
  - Status bar shows 9:41 AM and full battery (Apple convention, used universally)
  - No notch/cutout if the screenshots are for Play Store (Android usually has a status bar but no notch requirement)
  - PNG, sRGB, device-native portrait orientation
  - Recommended dimensions: 1080 x 1920 px minimum (Play Store minimum is 320 px on the short side, but tiny screenshots hurt conversion — go big)
  - Don't include personally identifying info about real people in any screenshot — use plausible fictional names ("Sam", "Jordan")
  - Avoid showing the user's real email, phone number, or contacts in any screenshot
