Tono is the AI tone coach that lives inside your keyboard. Draft a message, tap Coach, and Tono rewrites it on four axes — warmer, clearer, funnier, safer — with a risk badge so you know what might land badly before you hit send. One tap to swap in the version you actually meant.

Built for the messages you draft and stare at: the cold outreach you don't want to sound desperate in. The feedback to a direct report you don't want to sound harsh in. The text to your partner you don't want to sound defensive in. The message in a hard moment you don't want to send wrong.

WHY TONO
• Pre-send rewrites, not post-send regrets. You see the alternatives before anyone reads the message.
• Four tone axes, not one "make it nicer" button. Warmer / Clearer / Funnier / Safer — pick the register that matches the recipient.
• Risk badge flags messages likely to read as aggressive, passive-aggressive, or unclear — before you send.
• Style memory learns your voice over time so rewrites sound like you, not a chatbot.
• Per-recipient coaching adapts to the person you're writing to.
• Works in any app that uses the keyboard — Messages, WhatsApp, Gmail, Slack, LinkedIn, Notes, browser forms, anywhere.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Enable Tono as a keyboard in Settings → System → Languages & Input → On-screen keyboard.
2. Open any app and start typing. Draft your message as you normally would.
3. Tap the Coach button. Tono returns a risk score plus 4 rewrites.
4. Tap the rewrite you want. It replaces your draft in the input field. Send.

WHAT YOU STAY IN CONTROL OF
• Your draft text is sent to Tono's server for analysis and immediately discarded. We never store message content.
• You choose which rewrite — if any — to insert.
• "Help improve Tono" is opt-out. Off by default is one toggle away in Settings.
• Style memory and per-recipient profiles live on your device.
