FEATURE GRAPHIC — TONO (1024x500 PNG)

CONCEPT: A single iMessage-style chat bubble dominates the center of the canvas. Inside the bubble: a draft message in plain sans-serif (e.g., "Sorry for the late reply — I should have gotten back to you sooner."). To the right of the bubble, three "rewrite" bubbles fan out at slight angles, each in a distinct accent color:
  - Warmer (soft coral)
  - Clearer (cool blue)
  - Safer (muted green)

A small risk badge floats above the original bubble: a circle with "RISK: LOW" or a subtle "✓" check mark.

Above the chat bubbles, large bold headline (top-left aligned): "SAY WHAT YOU MEAN."
Below the bubbles, smaller text: "Land how you intend."

BACKGROUND: Soft off-white (#F7F7F5) or very light gradient from top to bottom — not pure white, not dark. The point is to feel like a calm writing surface.

BRAND: Small Tono wordmark in bottom-right corner. No Tono app icon on the feature graphic — the icon lives in the app listing separately.

TYPOGRAPHY: Modern sans-serif (Inter, SF Pro, or similar). Headline at ~72pt bold. Sub-headline at ~28pt regular. Message text inside bubbles at ~32pt. High contrast, accessibility-friendly.

DO NOT INCLUDE: Phone mockups, screenshots, hands, gradient mesh backgrounds, AI brain imagery, generic stock illustrations, more than three rewrite suggestions (cluttered), pricing text.

REASONING: Play Store feature graphics render at many sizes — readability at 1:1 on desktop and tiny on mobile. One bubble + three rewrites + one short headline works at every size. The chat-bubble metaphor immediately communicates "this is about messages" without requiring the user to read.

COLOR PALETTE (suggested):
  - Background: #F7F7F5
  - Primary text: #1A1A1A
  - Original bubble: white with #1A1A1A border
  - Warmer: #F4A89A
  - Clearer: #7AA9D9
  - Safer: #8FBF9C
  - Risk badge: #1A1A1A background, #FFFFFF text

PRODUCTION NOTES:
  - Generate at 1024x500 px
  - Keep all critical content within the safe zone (avoid 24px margin on all sides — Play Store may crop edges)
  - Export as PNG, sRGB, no alpha channel
  - File size should be under 1 MB for Play Store (recommend ~150-300 KB)
