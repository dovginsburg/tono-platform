# Screenshot Plan — Tono App Store listing

App Store requires 6.5" iPhone screenshots (iPhone 15 Pro Max or larger
equivalent). At least 3, up to 10. Captions and visual briefs below.

## Screenshot 1 — Hero / tagline

- **Headline:** Say what you mean. Land how you intend.
- **Visual:** Dark mode home screen of the Tono host app. Big tagline top,
  4 step cards below, no other UI.
- **Why:** Sets the brand and the value prop in 1 second.

## Screenshot 2 — The Coach flow

- **Headline:** Tap Coach. See rewrites.
- **Visual:** Split screen. Left half: a Messages thread with a draft
  typed. Right half: Tono keyboard in the result state — risk badge
  "Medium" in yellow, perception line, then 4 rewrite chips stacked.
- **Why:** This is the product. Anyone who gets the picture here gets
  the product.

## Screenshot 3 — Four axes

- **Headline:** Warmer. Clearer. Funnier. Safer.
- **Visual:** Close-up of 4 rewrite chips on the Tono keyboard, each with
  its glyph (warm.fill / sparkle / face.smiling / checkmark.shield) and
  a short example text.
- **Why:** Concrete value. Shows what the axes actually do.

## Screenshot 4 — Risk badge

- **Headline:** Know how it'll land — before you hit send.
- **Visual:** Side-by-side of 3 risk badges (low green / medium yellow /
  high red) each with a one-line perception and 1-2 example drafts that
  trigger them.
- **Why:** Risk badge is the unique differentiator vs Grammarly. Worth
  its own screenshot.

## Screenshot 5 — Style memory

- **Headline:** Learns how you talk.
- **Visual:** Settings tab, "Preferred voice" field filled in ("direct,
  terse, no thanks-bombs"), with a small "Tono applied this 12 times"
  counter.
- **Why:** Reassures users the rewrites will sound like them, not like
  an LLM.

## Screenshot 6 — Privacy

- **Headline:** No server. Your key. Your data.
- **Visual:** Settings → Provider row showing OpenAI / Anthropic / Mock,
  with a small "API key stored locally" badge.
- **Why:** Defuses the #1 App Store review objection for AI keyboards.

## Optional screenshots (7–10)

- **Screenshot 7 — Playground tab.** In-app playground showing the same
  draft analyzed live, useful for users who haven't enabled the keyboard
  yet.
- **Screenshot 8 — Pricing.** StoreKit-backed card: eligible 7-day trial, then
  $3.99/month or $39.99/year.
- **Screenshot 9 — Onboarding step 2 ("Allow Full Access").** Shows the
  iOS Settings path; defuses Full Access fear.
- **Screenshot 10 — ND-friendly framing.** Soft hero copy: "Built with
  ADHD adults in mind." Partner with ND creators for this one — it
  performs 3x better on TikTok.
