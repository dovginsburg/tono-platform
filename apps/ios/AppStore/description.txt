Say what you mean. Land how you intend.

Tono is an iOS keyboard that reads your message before you send it — and instantly offers four rewrites: warmer, clearer, funnier, or safer. No more wondering whether your email sounds passive-aggressive or your text reads as cold.

─────────────────────────
HOW IT WORKS
─────────────────────────
Type your message in any app, switch to the Tono keyboard, tap Coach, and get:

• A risk badge (Low / Medium / High) with a plain-English read of how your message lands
• One-tap rewrite chips — swap in the best version with a single tap
• Your original message stays intact until you choose to replace it

─────────────────────────
FOUR REWRITE AXES
─────────────────────────
Warmer — adds validation and warmth without changing your ask
Clearer — names the deadline or action item so the reader knows what to do
Funnier — lightens the tone without losing the point
Safer — removes phrases that read as passive, cold, or aggressive

─────────────────────────
BUILT FOR REAL CONVERSATIONS
─────────────────────────
Works in any app that shows a keyboard: Mail, Messages, Gmail, Slack, WhatsApp, LinkedIn, Notion — everywhere.

Recipient memory lets you save people with a voice hint ("boss — formal, no humor") so rewrites adapt to who you're talking to. Import from Contacts or add them manually.

Style memory learns which axes you tap most and surfaces those first.

─────────────────────────
WORKS OFFLINE
─────────────────────────
No signal? The keyboard falls back to a local analyzer so you still get a risk read and basic rewrites.

─────────────────────────
PRIVACY BY DESIGN
─────────────────────────
Your drafts are analyzed and immediately discarded. Tono never stores message content. Your account is an anonymous device ID — no email, no phone number, no sign-up.

─────────────────────────
FREE & PRO
─────────────────────────
