Version 1.0 — Launch

The Tono keyboard is here.

Type any message, tap Coach, and instantly see whether it might land the wrong way — plus four alternatives that fix it.

• Risk analysis (Low / Medium / High) with a one-line perception read
• Four axis rewrites: Warmer, Clearer, Funnier, Safer — one tap to insert
• Coach history: last 5 analyses available in the keyboard
• Recipient memory: save people with voice hints so rewrites adapt
• Import contacts to build your recipient list in seconds
• Style memory: your most-tapped axes move to the top automatically
• Offline mode: local analysis when there's no connection
• Widget: track your daily rewrite count from the home screen
• Share Extension: analyze any text from the share sheet
• Siri Shortcuts: "Coach my draft with Tono"
• Daily nudge (opt-in): gentle reminder on days you haven't coached
